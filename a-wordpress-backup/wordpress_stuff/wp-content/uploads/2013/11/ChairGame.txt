BackStory:



Once upon a time a boy lived happily with his family.

That was until the accident.......

The Chair Massacre!!!!!!!

Everyone in his family except for him was brutally murdered by the dining room chairs.

From that day onward he swore to destroy every chair he saw.






Mechanics:


use arrow keys or wasd to move. use z and x / j and k to attack.
Fight chairs that slowly increase in power as the evil chair manufacturer "Chair Co" increases in power. 
Fight their most expensive models and eventually fight the Chair God itself.

You kill chairs by attacking them. They drop coins. You purchase weapons and items. AND KILL ALL TEH CHAIRS
